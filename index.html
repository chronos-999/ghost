How to Get Bitcoin (BTC)

We only accept Bitcoin (BTC) for all services. Here's a complete guide to acquiring Bitcoin securely and anonymously.

1. Cryptocurrency Exchanges

Recommended Platforms:

  • • Kraken - High liquidity, competitive fees (0.16%-0.26%)
  • • Coinbase - Beginner-friendly, 1.49% + network fees
  • • Gemini - Secure, 1.49% maker, 2.49% taker
  • • Bitstamp - Reliable, 0.5% fees for high volume

Process: Sign up → Verify ID → Add payment method → Buy BTC → Withdraw to your wallet

2. Privacy-Focused Exchanges

For Anonymous Purchases:

  • • Monero.com - No KYC required, decentralized
  • • Bisq - P2P trading, complete privacy
  • • LocalBitcoins - Direct peer-to-peer, cash options
  • • Hodl Hodl - Non-custodial marketplace

Advantage: Minimal verification requirements, greater anonymity

3. Payment Methods

Common Ways to Buy:

  • • Bank Transfer - Lowest fees (0.1%-0.5%), slower (1-3 days)
  • • Credit/Debit Card - Instant, higher fees (2%-5%)
  • • PayPal - Quick, fees ~3.49%
  • • Cash - Anonymous, P2P platforms only

Recommendation: Bank transfers for cost-efficiency, cards for speed

4. Secure Wallet Setup

Types of Wallets:

  • • Hardware Wallets - Ledger, Trezor (most secure, $50-100)
  • • Cold Wallets - Electrum, Blue Wallet (offline storage)
  • • Mobile Wallets - Trust Wallet, Mycelium (convenient)

Best Practice: Hardware wallet for large amounts, mobile for transactions

5. Pro Tips

  • • Buy During Dips: Monitor price, purchase during downturns
  • • Fee Strategy: Batch purchases, use limit orders
  • • Security: Enable 2FA on all accounts, use unique passwords
  • • Tax Aware: Keep records for tax purposes (varies by jurisdiction)
  • • Testing: Send small amount first before large transfers

6. What to Avoid

  • • ❌ Sketchy exchanges with no verification
  • • ❌ Storing large amounts on exchange wallets
  • • ❌ Public WiFi for crypto transactions
  • • ❌ Clicking suspicious links or emails
  • • ❌ Sharing private keys or seed phrases
